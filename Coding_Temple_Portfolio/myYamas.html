Yama: Translation - Restraint

This stage of the path focuses on exercises, and practices as well as obstacles, or barriers that are internal or external. The goal of acknowledging the importance of Yama or "restraint" is to find the areas in one's life that arise with a sense of opposition or limitation. Ultimately, these are oppurtunities in disguise. A good example of this can be aspects of fitness or education with a paramount emphasis on health and sustainable growth.
